MONEY TRACKER

Money Laundering Detection Tool

Problem :- We are facing

In our bank,trillions of transactions happens,out of which millions of suspicious cases are generated.We at HSBC,have been monitoring and investigating Anti Money Laundering in the same way for past two decades and we have all experienced very high false positive rates and low (sometimes zero) disclosure rates to the regulator.

By use of Machine Intelligence techniques,We can reduce large amount of false positives and thus we can focus our approach to fight against financial crime and various illegal activities.

Hello

Our Objective

We are here to shoulder the responsibility to protect our bank from Regulatory,Reputational and Financial Risk and thus saves our bank from spending millions of dollars.We aims to monitor transactions effectively and thus detecting money laundering cases early.

Our tool goes through following stages:-Starting from Data Segmentation and Cleaning,then proceeding to Machine Learning and generating a model for classifying a case to be suspicious.

Feature selection is also performed through use of Python algorithms to identify the important parameters which plays significant role for final decision.Features are also visualised through 3D Visualisation techniques.
